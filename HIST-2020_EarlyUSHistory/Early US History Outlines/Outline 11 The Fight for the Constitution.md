## THE FIGHT FOR THE CONSTITUTION

## I. The Constitutional Convention

	- **A.** **==Founding Fathers==** **Meet in Philadelphia 1787**
	- 55 Delegates from 12 States
	- Most were Wealthy, Young, Experienced in the Law
	- Meetings Held in Secret
	- **B. Stronger National Government**
	- Powers to:-Tax
	- Regulate Trade
	- Maintain Army and Navy
	- **C. Two Major Compromises**
	- ==The Great Compromise== (Small States v Large States)
	- ==The 3/5ths Compromise== (Slave States v Free States)
