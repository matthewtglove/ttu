## THE ARTICLES OF CONFEDERATION

## I. Post-Revolutionary Reform

	- **A.** **==Gradual Emancipation==** **in the North**
	- ==African Methodist Episcopal (AME) Church== (1787)
	- **B.** **==Manumission==** **in the South**
	- John Laurens, M. De Lafayette
	- **C. Women’s Rights**
	- ==Abigail Adams==

## II. The First American Government

	- **A. Thirteen Sovereign States**
	- **B. Limited Central Power**
	- No Taxation
	- No Military
	- No Diplomacy
	- No Executive
	- **C. The** **==Northwest Ordinance==**
	- Provides for Addition of Future States
	- Banned Slavery North of Ohio River

## III. Problems with the Articles

	- **A. Violence on the Frontier**
	- Britain, Spain Continue to Fund Indian Attacks
	- **B. American Shipping and Trade Diminished**
	- No Longer Protected by the Royal Navy
	- Cut off from Triangular Trade
	- **C. Debt**
	- The Flow of Money from Europe Threatens to Dry up
	- **D.** **==Shays's Rebellion==** **(1786)**
	- Farmers, Dockworkers Terrorize Massachusetts
